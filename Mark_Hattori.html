Mark Hattori

Mark Hattori is a Japanese film and animation producer. Best known for his film production of "The Yokai King" and his animation "Gutsy Frog," and most recently a children's animation series, Hattori is up for making cutting-edge anime. In a bold move that underscores his dedication to Japanese cinema's heritage and his ambition to bring unseen stories to light, Mark Hattori has recently optioned the rights to produce the unmade movie scripts of Akira Kurosawa, one of Japan's most historically famous filmmakers of all time. This venture represents not only a significant professional milestone for Hattori but also a profound homage to Kurosawa's continued legacy. By bringing these unmade scripts to life, Hattori and Dime Novel Films aim to honor Kurosawa's artistic vision while introducing his timeless narratives to new generations of viewers.
